How Can We Help You?

Welcome to the NamasteYatri Help Center! We're here to assist you with any questions or concerns you may have while planning your trips or using our services. Browse our frequently asked questions (FAQs) below or contact our support team for further assistance.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. How do I book a trip through NamasteYatri?

Booking a trip on NamasteYatri is simple! Just go to our Destinations or Trips section, select your desired location, choose your travel dates, and click on Book Now. Follow the instructions to complete your booking, and you'll receive a confirmation email shortly.

2. Can I cancel or modify my booking?

Yes, you can cancel or modify your booking by going to the My Trips section in your account. Look for the trip you want to cancel or change, and select the appropriate option. Please note that cancellation and modification policies vary based on the trip and provider, so make sure to review the terms during booking.

3. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept a wide range of payment methods, including credit cards, debit cards, PayPal, and digital wallets. You can view all available payment options during the checkout process.

4. Is it safe to travel during the COVID-19 pandemic?

NamasteYatri prioritizes your safety. We follow the latest travel advisories and recommendations provided by health authorities. Please ensure you review our Travel Guidelines and check the local regulations of your travel destination before booking your trip.

5. How do I contact customer support?

You can reach out to our customer support team via email at support@namasteyatri.com or by calling our 24/7 hotline at +123-456-7890. We're here to assist you with any inquiries.
